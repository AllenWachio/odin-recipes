Lasagna

Lasagna is a layered Italian casserole made with sheets of pasta, rich meat sauce, creamy ricotta cheese mixture, and melted mozzarella and Parmesan. It's baked until bubbly and golden, offering a delicious combination of textures and flavors in every bite. Perfect for family gatherings or special occasions.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1/2 lb Italian sausage
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 (28 oz) can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 (6 oz) cans tomato paste
  • 1 (15 oz) can tomato sauce
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 tablespoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 3 cups ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 3 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 12 lasagna noodles

Procedure

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a large skillet, cook ground beef, Italian sausage, onion, and garlic until meat is browned. Drain fat.
  3. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, tomato sauce, water, sugar, basil, oregano, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 30 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  4. In a mixing bowl, combine ricotta cheese with the egg.
  5. Cook lasagna noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  6. Spread a thin layer of meat sauce in the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish. Place a layer of noodles on top.
  7. Spread a portion of the ricotta mixture over the noodles, then sprinkle with mozzarella and Parmesan cheese. Add more meat sauce over the cheese.
  8. Repeat layers, ending with meat sauce and a final layer of mozzarella and Parmesan cheese on top.
  9. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes, or until cheese is bubbly and slightly browned.
  10. Let the lasagna rest for about 15 minutes before serving.
